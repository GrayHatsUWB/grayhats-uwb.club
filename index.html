Who We Are

We're a group of people whose interest run the gamut. One thing that we can all agree on, however, is that technology is powerful. It can be used for good or for evil. We do our best to make sure that we leverage our skills to help instead of hurt. To join us, you don't have to be tech savvy, you just need a desire to learn and provoke change!

Additionally we love to compete in Cyber Defense competitions and CTFs. Every meeting, we learn something new from one another. If you're curious, check us out! We welcome all.
