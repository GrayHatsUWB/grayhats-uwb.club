Gray Hats

Who We Are

We're a group people whose interest run the gamut. However one thing that we agree upon is that technology is powerful. It can be used for good or for evil. We do our best to make sure that we leverage our skills to help instead of hurt. To join us you don't have to be technologically savy. You just need a desire to learn and provoke change.

Additionally we love to compete in Cyber Defense competitions and CTFs. Every meeting we are learning something new from one another. If you're curious check us out! We welcome all.
