Challenge Yourself

The Gray Hats Cyber Defense Competition places a team of Cyber Defense Specialists (Blue Team) in an unknown and highly vulnerable network with an Advanced Persistent Threat (Red Team) breathing down their back. Their job is to remediate network problems and insure the up-time of services such as web, email and file share.
