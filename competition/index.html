Competition

How it will work

This competition attempts to simulate a real world scenario in which a company fires their entire IT Staff (It happens. Boss is having a bad day, you know :p) and a incident response team must walk in with have little to know knowledge of the network lay out before hand and must fix it before the network is completely compromised by hackers. These scenarios are incredibly valuable to practice and experience in a safe environment so that way when you get out into the real world, you are better prepared for what you have to face. The competition operates concerning multiple teams.

Red Team

The Red Team is the Advance Persistent Threat. They are the bad guys trying to break into the network. They will do their hardest to make your lives miserable but at the same time provide meaningful learning experiences.

Blue Team

The Blue Team acts as the defenders of the network and company. They are in charge of maintaining services online such as web, email and file share.

Orange Team

The Orange Team Simulates customers and employee's of the scenarios company.

White Team

The White Team consists of the team Team judges. They make sure the different teams obey the different rules.

Black Team

The Black Team is the infrastructure Team. Their job is to make sure everything is running smoothly and that there are no **unintended** network outages.

Gold Team

The Gold Team is your bosses, bosses, boss. Listen to them. They have the final word/say regarding all manners.
